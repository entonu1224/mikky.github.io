About us

Mikky Books Enterprise is a publishing company located at No 4 Baba Onoja Close Off Atakpa Road Ohimini, Zone C Benue State Nigeria in West Africa. Publishing across different genre. As a company, we are committed to improving literacy in Africa and the world at large. We are also passionate about inspiring the society to positive change. We support communities through talent hunt, book donations and other local activities. We work with authors and help them find the balance that they required to express their creativity. We published books that will have great impact on the world for ages. We are relatively new but we look forward to becoming one of the top publishing company in the world.
